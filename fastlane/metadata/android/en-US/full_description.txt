Launch is a clean, efficient, and minimalist Android launcher designed to maximize simplicity and productivity.

---

🧠 Smart App Management
- Sorts apps by usage frequency
- Rarely used apps grouped alphabetically
- App usage time shown beside icons
- Weekly usage summary at the bottom
- One-tap uninstall via long press
- Switch between grid and list view

---

🔍 Smart Universal Search
- Search apps, contacts, and web
- Built-in calculator in search bar
- Quick links to Play Store, YouTube, Maps, and Browser
- Tap to call, SMS, or WhatsApp contacts from results

---

⚡ Quick Actions
- Time widget → Clock  
- Date widget → Calendar  
- Search bar:  
  • Long press → Google search in browser  
  • Double tap → Change wallpaper  
- Weather widget with live data and API key support

---

🔖 Dock Customization
- Add, rename, or group favorite apps
- Customize number of dock icons and layout

---

🚫 Focus Mode
- Temporarily hide distracting apps
- Whitelist allowed apps
- Set a countdown timer
- Cannot exit until timer ends

---

🔐 App Lock
- Lock individual apps with PIN or fingerprint
- 1-minute timeout window after unlocking
- Protect apps like WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.

---

✅ To-do List Widget
- Add tasks with due time and priority
- Supports daily, weekly, or custom recurring tasks
- Visual completion tracking on home screen

---

💰 Finance Tracker
- Track income and expenses
- Add notes to transactions
- Monthly savings overview

---

⚙️ Settings
- Backup and restore all data
- Reset usage stats any time
- Toggle grid/list layout
- Change weather API key
- Manage app locks

---

🔋 Power Saver Mode
- Keeps wallpaper black to save battery
- Hides app usage stats and weather updates
- Ideal for low-power or OLED displays

---

🤏 Gestures
- Tap or long-press widgets for quick actions
- Long-press icons for uninstall or rename
- Calculator in search bar

---

🎙️ Voice Commands
Use your voice in the search bar:
- Call Swaroop
- Search weather
- Uninstall WhatsApp
- Open YouTube

---

🔐 Privacy
Launch is 100% open source. No ads. No tracking.

Permissions used:
- Contacts → for search
- Phone & SMS → for quick actions
- Storage → for notes, wallpapers, and backups
- Location → for weather (OpenWeatherMap)
- Microphone → for voice input
