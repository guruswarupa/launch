Launch — Minimal Productivity Launcher

A clean, fast, minimalist Android launcher built for focus and productivity.

Replace your cluttered home screen with a simple interface that helps you stay organized, reduce distractions, and get things done.

Open source • No ads • No tracking • Privacy first

🔍 Smart App Management

Understand how you use your phone.

• Apps automatically sorted by usage frequency
• Daily & weekly usage charts
• Per-app daily time limits — icons gray out when limits are reached
• Quick session timers (1, 5, 10 minutes)

🔕 Focus Mode

Stay distraction-free.

• Hide distracting apps instantly
• Countdown focus timer that cannot be exited early
• Automatic Do Not Disturb during focus sessions

Your data stays on your device.

• Encrypted vault for sensitive files (biometric + recovery key)

⚠️ Accessibility Service Disclosure

Launch uses the Android Accessibility Service only to enable specific productivity features requiring system interaction.

Used for:

Double-Tap to Lock
Lock the device by double-tapping the home screen.

Screenshot Shortcut
Capture screenshots using a quick gesture.

Control Center Quick Toggles
Allows toggling system settings like Wi-Fi, Do Not Disturb, and flashlight from the Control Center overlay, accessible from any screen.

The Accessibility Service is NOT used to:

• collect personal data
• read screen content
• monitor user activity
• modify settings without permission

All features are optional and can be disabled anytime in Settings.
